Test QR Codes
Relay URL wss://

Scans as a relay address. The app will offer to add it to your relay list.

wss://relay.damus.io
Deep Link — Add Relay nostrkey://

NostrKey deep link that triggers the add-relay flow directly.

nostrkey://add-relay?url=wss://nos.lol
Public Key (npub) npub1

A Nostr public key in bech32 format. The app will show the profile view.

npub1sg6plzptd64u62a878hep2kev3zw7gnway8eumg68nn7sflg6nyscmh755
NIP-46 Connect nostrconnect://

Remote signing request. The app will initiate a NIP-46 session with the requesting app.

nostrconnect://ab12cd34...?relay=wss://relay.damus.io&metadata={"name":"TestApp"}
Unknown Content plain text

Unrecognized content. The app will show the raw text in the unknown QR handler.

Hello from NostrKey test!

How to Use

Open the NostrKey iOS app, tap the Scanner tab, and point your camera at any QR code on this page to test each scan type.

Scan Types

  • wss:// Relay URL
  • nostrkey:// Deep Link
  • npub1 Public Key
  • nostrconnect:// NIP-46
  • Plain text (fallback)

nostrkey://add-relay

nostrkey://connect

nostrkey://import-keys
